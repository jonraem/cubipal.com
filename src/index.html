Our product

We are Cubipal, the creators of the inexpensive learning and prototyping platform. Our dream is to give people the possibility to learn programming and electronics without hassle and spending lots of money.

If you want to learn programming and embedded electronics, you would normally have to figure out which components to buy, how they work together and then assemble it all by yourself. Not to mention you would have to spend a ton of money on the components! With Cubipal all of these things are thought out for you. The device contains all the sensors you need so you can start testing it out right away. And Cubipal won't break your bank!

Some similar embedded prototyping platforms offer you many of the same things but they often cost hundreds of dollars. We have designed Cubipal to be an easy and inexpensive springboard to your learning!
